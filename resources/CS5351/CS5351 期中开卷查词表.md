### **<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">补充说明：i.e. vs. e.g.</span>**

*   ### **<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">i.e.</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"> 来自拉丁语 "id est"，意思是 "that is"。它用于</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">重述或解释</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">，表示“换句话说”。可以替换为 "namely" 或 "in other words"。</span>
*   ### **<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">e.g.</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"> 来自拉丁语 "exempli gratia"，意思是 "for example"。它用于</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">引出例子</span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)">。可以替换为 "for example" 或 "such as"。</span>

PM一般语境下是**产品经理**应该没有人类不知道吧，不是项目经理哈

> 然而在**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">“过程模型”和“项目管理”</span></span>**这个语境下，一般指的是“项目经理”，因为<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">在敏捷开发中，</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">Product Manager（产品经理）</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)"> 的职责通常由 </span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">Product Owner（产品负责人）</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)"> 这个角色来承担。Product Owner 的核心职责是：</span></span>
>
> *   **<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">管理和优先排序产品待办列表</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">（第二章PPT64页）</span></span>
>
> *   **<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">代表客户和利益相关者</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">，定义产品功能</span></span>
>
> *   <span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">在评审会上</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">验收完成的工作</span></span>**
>
> <span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">如果在题目描述中出现了</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">定义产品需求、决定功能优先级</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">等活动时使用了“PM”，那么它可能指代产品经理。</span></span>
>
> <span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">在专门介绍Scrum的部分（第二章PPT64页），课件明确列出了三个角色：</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">Scrum Master</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">, </span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">Product Owner</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">, 和 </span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">Scrum Development Team</span></span>**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)">。它特别注明：“In many projects, this role (Scrum Master) is assumed by the project manager.” 这段的意思是，</span></span>**Project Manager**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)"> 是一个可以承担 </span></span>**Scrum Master**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)"> 职责的职位，从而与 </span></span>**Product Owner**<span style="color: rgb(15, 17, 21)"><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255)"> 的职责分开。</span></span>
